Tips & Tricks

Try finding your clip on YouTube and post the link in to VideoGrabby to download it as an .mp3. Once that's done you can trim the file down to only the soundbyte you want easily by using Audacity, which is free and open source.

Simply drag and drop your sound file in to Audacity, highlight the bit you want, trim (Ctrl+T) and export (Ctrl + Shift + E) the file to the desired location. It is recommended that you export your files as .ogg considering that .mp3 is technically proprietary.
